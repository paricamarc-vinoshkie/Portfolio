03 — Mid-Project Breakage

3 problems. 3 clean fixes.

Real bookkeeping doesn't go in a straight line. Here's what broke, and the reconciled proof after each fix.

01

GST tax-inclusive trap

32 sales invoices were rejecting at reconciliation — GST was being added on top of the deposit instead of backed out of it. Fixed by recalculating every line at deposit ÷ 1.1.

02

Deleted bank statement, recovered

~50 transactions vanished mid-reconciliation. Instead of re-importing blind, each line was checked against real ledger status first — zero duplicated income or expense.

03

Orphaned payment links

30+ invoices reverted to "unpaid" while the cash side stayed correct. Diagnosed via Account Transactions, re-matched only the genuinely missing lines — no double-counting.
